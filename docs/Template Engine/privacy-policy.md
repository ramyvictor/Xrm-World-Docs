# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the Template Engine handles your data and information when used within your organization’s Power Platform environment.

## 1. Data Collection
- The Template Engine does not collect or transmit any personal data outside your organization’s environment.
- All data processed by the Template Engine remains within your Dataverse and Power Platform infrastructure.

## 2. Data Usage
- The Template Engine processes data as defined by your templates, triggers, and user actions.
- No data is shared with third parties by the Template Engine itself.

## 3. Data Security
- Data security is managed by your organization’s Power Platform and Dataverse security policies.
- Access to the Template Engine and its configuration should be restricted to authorized users.

## 4. User Responsibility
- Users are responsible for ensuring that templates and triggers do not expose or misuse sensitive information.
- Always follow your organization’s data protection and privacy guidelines when configuring and using the Template Engine.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
- This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of the Template Engine constitutes acceptance of the latest policy.

If you have questions or concerns about privacy, please contact your system administrator or data protection officer.
